Walleye Weekend Summer Festival

What bands are playing this year

Many bands are playing this year such as: Flannel Revival, FallBack, DJ Galaxy Driftwood, Suzie & The Detonators, Brent Bel & The Boys, Driftwood (headline set), and The Glam Band

Where do they play?

The bands will be spread out across three different stages throughout the weekend, with the largest performances taking place on the main stage and being held on Saturday.

Here is a quote from someone being asked who they are excited to watch, I can’t wait to see Glam Band again! They killed it last year!

Glam Band is a rock band that goes all out. Dressing up in flashy clothing, heavy makeup and wearing large wigs. They do covers for many bands like, Bon Jovi, Def Leppard, and Guns N’ Roses
